TRAVEL CEYLON

Colombo

Gangaramaya Temple

Gangaramaya Temple is one of the most important Buddhist temples in Colombo. Visitors can see the temple's unique blend of Sri Lankan, Thai, and Chinese architecture and explore its extensive collection of Buddhist artifacts.

National Museum of Colombo

The National Museum of Colombo is a fascinating museum that houses a vast collection of artifacts and exhibits, including traditional Sri Lankan masks, antique weapons, and ancient sculptures.

Galle Face Green

Galle Face Green is a popular urban park and promenade located in the heart of Colombo, Sri Lanka. The park stretches along the Indian Ocean, providing visitors with a panoramic view of the sea, and is a great place to unwind and relax after a long day of exploring the city.

Viharamahadevi Park

Viharamahadevi Park is a beautiful park located in the heart of the city, named after the queen who helped bring Buddhism to Sri Lanka. It features many walking paths, statues, and ponds, and is a great spot to relax and enjoy nature.

Mount Lavinia Beach

Mount Lavinia Beach is a popular spot for both locals and tourists to enjoy the sun, sand, and sea. It's a great place to swim, relax, or enjoy a delicious seafood meal at one of the many beachside restaurants.

Dehiwala Zoo

Dehiwala Zoo is a large zoo and aquarium that is home to a wide variety of animals, including elephants, tigers, and crocodiles. Visitors can enjoy a day of exploring the zoo's many exhibits and attractions.
