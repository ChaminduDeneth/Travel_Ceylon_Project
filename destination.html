Galle

Galle, located on the southwestern coast of Sri Lanka. The Galle Fort, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, is a must-visit attraction that boasts of colonial-era buildings, museums, and galleries. Other popular tourist destinations in Galle include the beautiful Unawatuna Beach and the lush green Rumassala Hill.

Kandy

Kandy, a picturesque city located in the central highlands of Sri Lanka, is famous for its cultural significance and natural beauty. The Sacred Temple of the Tooth Relic, the Royal Botanical Gardens, and the scenic Kandy Lake are some of the popular tourist destinations that attract visitors from around the world.

Colombo

Colombo, the vibrant capital of Sri Lanka, is a melting pot of diverse cultures and a hub for tourism. The city boasts of many popular tourist attractions, including the ancient Gangaramaya Temple, the picturesque Galle Face Green, and the bustling Pettah Market, offering an unforgettable experience for visitors.

Jaffna

Jaffna, a city located in the northern region of Sri Lanka, is a cultural and historical hub. Tourists can explore the Jaffna Fort, the Nallur Kandaswamy Kovil, and the Jaffna Public Library, which showcases the city's rich heritage. The beautiful Casuarina Beach is also a popular destination for tourists.

Hambanthota

Hambantota, located in the southern region of Sri Lanka, is a destination that offers a mix of natural beauty and cultural attractions. Visitors can explore the Yala National Park, the ancient Mulkirigala Rock Temple, and the Hambantota Salt Pans, which showcase the city's unique features. The picturesque beaches of Hambantota are also a popular destination for tourists.

Anuradhapura

Anuradhapura, located in the North Central Province of Sri Lanka, is an ancient city that showcases the country's rich cultural and religious heritage. Tourists can explore the Sacred City, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, which includes the famous Sri Maha Bodhi Tree and numerous ancient temples, stupas, and monuments. The city also offers a serene environment with beautiful lakes and parks.
